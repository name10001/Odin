Your Goal:

Be the first to remove all your cards.

Setup:

All players are dealt the same number of cards randomly from the deck.

The first top card in the discard pile is chosen randomly.


Playing Cards:

A card may only be played if it is compatible with the top card on the discard pile.

If you cannot play a card, you must pick up from the deck.


Compatibility:

In general, cards which share the same type OR colour are compatible.

Colour compatibility:

Red, blue, green or yellow cards are the default colours with no special rules.

Black cards are wild cards and can be used with any of the four default colours, but NOT purple cards.

White cards are also wild cards and can be used with the four default colours AND purple cards.

However, black and white cards are NOT compatibile with eachother.

Orange cards are NOT compatible with both black and white cards.

Rainbow cards are compatible with ALL cards.

Playing multiple cards:

You are allowed to play multiple cards on your turn.

In general, all cards played on your turn must be of the same type.

The last card you played at the end of your turn will become the new top card in the discard pile.


Pickup Chains:

Some cards will start a pickup chain when played. Such as +2, +4 or +10 cards.

During a pickup chain, only cards compatible with pickup chains may be played. Such as other +2, +4 or +10 cards, reverse, skip, x2 cards, etc.

A pickup chain has an associated value which increases as the pickup chain progresses. This value is determined by the cards that are played.

If a player cannot further the chain, they will be forced to pickup the pickup chain's value from the deck.

A chain may also end prematurely if someone uses a card that ends the chain. Such as a fuck you, plus or pawn card.
